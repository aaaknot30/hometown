Visit Colorado Springs

The Pikes Peak Region offers many attractions, including trains, museums, parks, a zoo and more.

Top three activities to do in Colorado Springs

Garden of the Gods

Public park located in Colorado Springs, Colorado. Red rock formations were created during a geological upheaval.

Manitou Incline Trail

Popular hiking trail rising above Manitou Springs, Colorado. The Incline is famous for its sweeping views and steep grade

The Broadmoor Hotel

The Broadmoor is a member of Historic Hotels of America of the National Trust for Historic Preservation.
